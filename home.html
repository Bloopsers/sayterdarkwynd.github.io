• Hi, I'm Chris DeChamplain and I love design.

    I dedicate my life to creative pursuits, using design to make the world a bit more beautiful.

    The world is in constant flux, bringing new technological innovations to the fore, and exploring these new facets of creativity excites me. I aim to help bring the future to people through creativity.

    My philosophy is simple: Make if fun. If the user isn't engaged and interested...what's the point?

  • My Specialities

  • Concepting

    Taking a vision and bringing it to life is one of my favorite things to do. It allows freedom of expression, and an opportunity to try new things.

  • Web Design

    Creating sites from wireframe concepts and making them accessible to users is critically important for the success of any modern business.

  • Worldbuilding

    Making a game isn't just about graphics or fancy physics. Worldbuilding is essential to bringing these digital universes to life.

  • CSS

    Without CSS you can't make a site all it can be, and I've got a good head for it. Responsive design is the future, and a wise designer knows it.

  • Graphic Design

    Brand Identity, social media presence and digital marketing can make or break a company. Don't get left in the dust.

  • Illustration

    A lot can be conveyed with a simple sketch, or a character design. I've done quite a bit of sketching, but my real forte is in colors.
