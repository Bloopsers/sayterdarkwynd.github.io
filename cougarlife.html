• Cougarlife

    Women are in charge

    A product of AvidLife, Cougarlife started pretty small when I first joined the team. After 6 years, our marketing had increased site presence substantially, leading to millions of new signups.

    Landing pages, storyboards for broadcast advertisements, front-end web design for the site and a great deal of digital marketing and Brand Idendity design were within the scope of my duties.

    Cougarlife was about being edgy and sexy, while keeping the overall impact heavily slanted towards women in their 30s and 40s. This resulted in some truly unique television ads, viral marketing campaigns and billboard designs.
