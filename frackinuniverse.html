• Frackin' Universe

    Perhaps the biggest mod ever made

    Perhaps my most ambitious gaming related project ever, Frackin' Universe started as a mod for Starbound with a single plant and ballooned out rapidly from there. It soon grew to become a whole new way to play the game and contains thousands of new items, more than 70 biomes the player can explore, vehicles, a complicated gene-crafting system to engineer flora and a new environmental effects system and a massive end-game dungeon with puzzles, a boss, and tons of lore (to name a few things).

    There are far too many features to list, and after 3 years of development it is by far the largest mod for the game and potentially the largest anywhere. With over 100,000 players, it has a little something for everyone.

    I've utilized every bit of skill I have to create this massive universe: programming, scripting, UI design, graphics,spreadsheets, charts, wireframes and more. Additionally, many have come and gone as team members during this time and I've acted as Creative Director on the project to keep things running smoothly.

    It's been noted and reviewed in established gaming sites such as Kotaku, Twinfinite, Rock, Paper, Shotgun, heaps of videos on YouTube and and the devs themselves
