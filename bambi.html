• Bambi

    Appealing to the Young

    Bambi was intended to be a service for young adults into the party nightlife. It would facilitate meetups, and much of the content would be paid for by male members looking to invite groups of female members for a night out.

    Bright color, good contrast and a hip design were chosen to appeal to a more modern and trendy crowd. The layout is both familiar and new, incporating elements people expect with just a hint of something fresh.
