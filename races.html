  • Avian

    Disadvantages: -15% Health, Weakness to Ice damage

    Advantages: +10% Energy, -65% Fall Damage, Slowed Fall, Jump Boost

    Mystic: 20% Damage bonus with Wands and Staves

    Avian Rage: 5.5% Damage bonus, with increasing damage the lower your health gets.

    Immunities: None

  • Floran

    Disadvantages: -10% Health, Weakness to Fire damage

    Advantages: +15% Energy

    Photosynthesis: When the sun is up, you slowly regenerate health

    Floran Warrior: 20% Bow damage increase, Spears increase defense when swung

    Floran Hunger: The hungrier you are, the more damage you do.

    Immunities: Shock

  • Glitch

    Disadvantages: Increased energy regen time, Weakness to Electric, Weakened when submerged

    Advantages: +5% Energy, Reduced Hunger rate, +15% Health, +3 Protection

    Automaton: Resting in oil pools will regenerate your health

    Arms Training: Perfect Blocks increase your damage in increments. Stackable

    Chivalry: 1h combos increase your defense

    Immunities: Poison, Bee Stings

  • Human

    Disadvantages: None

    Advantages: +15% Knockback, +30 Max Food level, +0.07 Jump Time

    Defensive: Perfect Blocks will increase your defense. Stackable

    Combative: 1h combos increase your damage

    Immunities: None

  • Hylotl

    Disadvantages: -20% Energy, Weakness to Electric

    Advantages: +10% Health

    Martial Training: Perfect Blocks regenerate health. Stackable

    Amphibious: When submerged gain +4 Protection, +20 Health, +1400 oxygen and increased swim speed

    Warrior Soul: Spears, Hammers and Axes increase defense when swung

    Immunities: Wet

  • Novakid

    Disadvantages: -10% Health, Weakness to Poison

    Advantages: +30% Energy, -25% Knockback, +5% Speed

    Star Child: Glow in the dark

    Gunslinger: +8% damage with all firearms

    Immunities: All radiation
