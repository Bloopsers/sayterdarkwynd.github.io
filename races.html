    At home in a jungle and able to walk with ease, the Apex are still physically adept despite the best efforts of the MiniKnog. They like things that go boom, making grenade launchers especially dangerous in their hands. Hammers are their favorite thing, and they zip about doing insane damage with them.

    They are just as happy using their animal instincts with fist weapons, and are tough to take out in a fistfight.

    +8% Speed, +15% Jump, +15% Health

    Hammer and Axe do an additional 20% damage. Increased Speed when using hammers

    Demolitionist

    Apex love explosions. When using Grenade Launchers, they do +20% Damage

    Jungle Fury

    +25% Damage and +2 Protection when using Fist weapons

    Immunities:

    Jungle Tiles, Mud Tiles

    Humans can typically last longer without food than the other races, and have wonderfully powerful legs that allow them to jump just a little longer than a few others. They are otherwise incredibly average, and a good baseline choice for a new player.

    Humans are naturally skilled at defending themselves with a shield. When you block perfectly, your defense is boosted even further! Pair that with a Shortsword, and you can really pack on the pain. If ranged combat is your style, humans are especially dangerous with Machine Pistols and Assault Rifles.

    Disadvantages

    None

    Advantages

    +30 Max Food level, +Jump Hold Time

    Defensive

    Perfect Blocks increase your defense (Stacks)

    Vengeance

    +20% Damage with Shortswords

    Precision

    +15% Damage with Assault Rifles and Machine Pistols

    Immunities:

    None

    While they prefer peace, most Hylotl are also trained in Martial Arts due to tradition in their culture. As a result, the art of swordplay is second nature to them. With other forms of weapons, they focus on defense and excel. Shields are well used too, and they are able to withstand blows and come out restored.

    Underwater, Hylotl are not to be trifled with, as they are tougher and faster there. They prefer to live underwater, and can hold their breath for a very, very long time.

    Advantages

    With no real culture of their own, the Novakids roam the galaxy as intrepid explorers. They are obsessed with earthen Wild West themes, and they each adore Pistols and Sniper Rifles as defensive and offensive tools. They are tough to push around, and fleet of foot.

    Whips are quite familiar to them due to cattle rustlin' and the like, and they can use them to great effect. Pair this with complete immunity to all radiation, and a natural ability to emit light and you've got a very useful explorer at your command!
