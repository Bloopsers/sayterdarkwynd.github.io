    Racial Perks

    When well fed, gain a substantial boost to your maximum Energy. However, such a bonus comes at a price:

    Failing to stay well fed will begin the de-evolution process. This drops your energy substantially but you gain Physical Resistance, Knockback Resistance and Health.

    Environmental Bonus

    In Jungles, Rainforests, Primeval Forests, Forests and Snow biomes you gain increased Health and Energy
