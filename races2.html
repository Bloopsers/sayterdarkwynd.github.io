    At home in a jungle and able to walk with ease, the Apex are still quite tied to their instincts despite the best efforts of the MiniKnog. Their primal side tends to present itself when they are hungering, for the conversion process has a high metabolic impact. The less you eat, the more sturdy you become.

    They are just as happy using their animal instincts with fist weapons, and are tough to take out in a fistfight. Don't think they're brutes, though. These people are cunning, intelligent and resourceful. So long as they keep fed, they are able to out-think most races with ease!

    When well fed, gain a substantial boost to your maximum Energy. However, such a bonus comes at a price:

    Failing to stay well fed will begin the de-evolution process. This drops your energy substantially but you gain Physical Resistance, Knockback Resistance and Health.
